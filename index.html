Scene 1 · The Moment of Truth

Pathfinder today vs Pathfinder with ATOM

Same Trusted Advisor, same customer, same hour. Completely different experience.

Today’s story

1. Advisor opens Pathfinder and walks through a 60+ minute IQA.

2. The submission vanishes into a 3–5 day engineering queue.

3. Someone spends 2–4 hours researching providers and rebuilding a matrix.

4. A static PDF and recommendation appear in the advisor’s inbox.

5. The advisor tries to re‑ignite the customer conversation days later.

The ATOM story

1. As the IQA starts, ATOM quietly begins listening and structuring the session.

2. A small side panel surfaces next questions, phrasing, and objections.

3. Dynamic Matrices fill in and re‑rank in real time as answers land.

4. By minute 25, a ranked recommendation and story arc are already drafted.

5. The call ends with a clear path and a customer‑ready artifact, not a todo.

Dynamic Matrices that move with the conversation

Explainable by design

Scene 4 · Under the Hood

Pathfinder stays the system of record. ATOM becomes the brain.

The architecture is simple on purpose: Pathfinder owns the workflow and data; ATOM adds an evidence‑first, session‑centric intelligence layer.

Pathfinder

Handles log‑in, IQA configuration, submissions, routing, and UI – the front door and the record of everything that happens.

ATOM services

Listen to IQAs and calls, normalize them, retrieve the right provider facts, score fit, coach the advisor, and assemble artifacts in the background.

Knowledge & providers

Provider profiles, Dynamic Matrices, Atlas data, case studies, and compliance details live as a governed knowledge base – the ground truth ATOM references.

Security & control

Encryption, roles, audit, and redaction follow familiar enterprise patterns. ATOM plugs into what Avant already does well, instead of inventing a new island.

Underneath, this is powered by the same ATOM agentic platform Antimatter uses for other enterprises: Agentic AI.
